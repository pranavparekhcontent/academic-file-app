Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Academic File App free to use?

Yes! It is a 100% free Progressive Web Application designed for degree, pharmacy, and diploma college faculty members.

How does it sync with Smart Attendance?

It connects to your institution's central Google Apps Script backend and matches topic headers from Smart Attendance to your teaching plan sheet.

Can I download reports as Microsoft Word (.docx) files?

Yes. The app features a custom client-side OOXML compiler that generates formatted .docx files complete with college headers and signature lines.

Does it work offline on mobile and desktop?

Yes! As a PWA, it can be installed on Android, iOS, Windows, and macOS with full offline caching support.
